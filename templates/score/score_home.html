My musical development

If you've been following my musescore account for a while (don't worry if you haven't), you might have noticed a rather significant change wrt the genres of music I've been working on, over time. I started with Jazz, focusing on the big band/swing style, then slowly leaning towards concert band, focusing on the instrumentalization of pop and film music. Right now I'm into marching/military, as well as the 70s-80s jazz fusion, concentrating in the Asian areas such as Japan.

Ever since I was introduced to music - this is years before 2017 - I've been an "instrumental over vocal" person. While I still have mandopop/j-pop/etc favorites such as Jay Chou and Jackie Chan (yes, he makes good music as well), pure instrumentation has always been my go-to choice.

No doubt that my taste is still evolving as I'm exposed to more musical styles, variants of exisiting styles, and technical knowledge, but as of now the above is roughly what happened over these 5 years.
